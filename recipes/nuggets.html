Chicken Nuggies & Fries


Ingredients

  • Frozen Chicken Nuggets
  • Frozen French Fries
  • Dipping Sauce (I prefer ranch)

Directions

  1. Put chicken nuggets and french fries on plate.
  2. Microwave nugs and fries to desired temperature.
  3. Pour some of your favorite dipping sauce.
  4. Enjoy!
