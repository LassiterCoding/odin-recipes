PB&J


Ingredients

  • 2 Pieces of Bread
  • Jar of Peanut Butter
  • Jar of Grape Jelly
  • Butter Knife (for spreading)

Directions

  1. Lay two slices of bread flat on the counter.
  2. Open jar of peanut butter and scoop out desired amount with your butter knife.
  3. Spread peanut butter across one slice of the bread.
  4. Repeat steps 2 & 3 with your grape jelly and the other slice of bread.
  5. Smash both slices of bread together with the peanut butter and jelly in the middle.
  6. Enjoy!
