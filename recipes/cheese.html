Grilled Cheese & Soup


Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of Bread
  • 2 Slices of Cheese
  • Butter
  • 1 Can of Soup

Directions

  1. Lay two slices of bread on the counter.
  2. Place a piece of cheese on each slice and make the sandwich.
  3. Melt butter in a hot pan and place sandwich in pan.
  4. Wait until bottom side is golden brown and then flip.
  5. Wait until bottom side is golden brown and cheese is melty, and remove from pan.
  6. Pour soup into bowl and microwave.
  7. Enjoy!
